About

My interest in web development originates from my appreciation for the internet. Growing up in the 90's, I was able to witness the dot com boom in the "user" perspective. From the days of dial-up to wireless connection, user experience has grown increasingly more interactive. Impressed by the ever evolving user experience and packed with a decades worth of user experience, I am at the point where I intend to dig a bit deeper and expand my understanding of this phenomena as a Full Stack Developer.
